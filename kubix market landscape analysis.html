⚡ Pain Points & Decision Drivers
😰 Fear of Market Scams

Thai investors are highly sensitive to investment fraud

✅ Emphasize SEC-approval, backed by real commodity
🤔 Don't Understand Digital Tokens

Low familiarity with tokenized investments

✅ Visual, short-form explainers: "ลงทุนยังไง ได้อะไร คืนเมื่อไหร่"
📊 No Clear ROI Timeline

Want transparent return expectations

✅ Show Fixed % p.a. + Bonus Timeline in plain Thai
🔒 Concern About Lock-in

Worried about liquidity and exit options

✅ Clarify "ถือ 3 ปี = ได้โบนัส" vs "ขายก่อนก็ได้แต่…"
🏢 Too Many New Platforms

Platform fatigue and trust issues

✅ Position Kubix as trusted, top-tier ICO portal
💰 Low Financial Literacy

Need education-first approach

✅ Focus on tangible backing (commodities > abstract concepts)
